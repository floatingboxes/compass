Tips & Tricks

Fall is a great time to plant some of those shrubs, trees, and perennials you've had your eye on. Freshen things up with a fall cleanup, spade edging of planting beds, and a fresh layer of our premium double shredded hardwood mulch. Adjust your mower blade height to 2.5". Fall is also a great time to de-thatch/aerate and overseed your lawn with our high quality seed. Cut back perennials for winter, unless they have a winter interest. Apply winter protection to your roses and perennials.Remember to plant your spring bulbs. To add color for the fall, plant mums and pansies. There are a variety of colors that help enhance your home's curb appeal.
